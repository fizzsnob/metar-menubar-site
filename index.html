METAR MenuBar

Color-coded FAA flight categories in your Mac menu bar.

A small macOS app that shows the current flight category (VFR, MVFR, IFR, LIFR) for one or more airports as a dot in your menu bar. Click for a fully-decoded METAR observation.

⚠️ Not for navigation or flight planning. METAR MenuBar is an informational convenience tool. It is not a substitute for an official FAA preflight weather briefing. Always obtain a complete briefing from an authorized source — Leidos Flight Service (1-800-WX-BRIEF / 1800wxbrief.com) or the FAA Aviation Weather Center (aviationweather.gov) — before every flight.
